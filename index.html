Who We Are

Rooted in Tradition, Driven by Innovation

At AS AGRICULTURE LLC, we represent the nexus of modern agronomy and responsible land stewardship. Founded with a vision to redefine yield potential without compromising the environment, we specialize in diverse crop farming and forestry management across California and beyond.

Our philosophy is simple: the soil is a living asset. We employ data-driven techniques to maximize productivity in crop production while simultaneously preserving the biodiversity of the ecosystems we manage.

With decades of collective experience in the agriculture, fishing, and hunting sectors, our team brings a holistic approach to resource management. We don't just grow crops; we cultivate sustainable cycles that benefit landowners, communities, and the planet.

From precision irrigation systems to forestry conservation planning, AS AGRICULTURE LLC stands as a partner in long-term agricultural success.

Our Expertise

Comprehensive Agricultural Solutions

Specialty Crop Farming

We leverage advanced agronomic practices to cultivate high-value crops tailored to specific microclimates. Our methods ensure optimal soil health and maximizing yield per acre through rotational diversity. We handle everything from seed selection to harvest logistics.

Forestry & Land Management

Sustainable timber harvesting and forest regeneration are at the core of our operations. We provide comprehensive land management services that balance economic return with wildlife habitat preservation. Our team navigates complex regulatory environments to ensure full compliance.

Resource Consulting

Strategic planning for agricultural enterprises looking to modernize their operations. We offer consultancy on water usage efficiency, soil amendments, and crop rotation schedules. Our goal is to reduce operational overhead while increasing overall farm output.
